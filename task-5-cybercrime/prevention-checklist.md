# Stay Safe Online -- Prevention Checklist

## 🔐 General Safety Tips

-   Never share your passwords, OTPs, or UPI PIN with anyone.
-   Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all important accounts.
-   Verify links and websites before clicking---look for HTTPS and
    correct URLs.
-   Keep your apps and devices updated to protect against
    vulnerabilities.
-   Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions or logins.
-   Be cautious of urgent or "too good to be true" offers and messages.

## 💰 Financial / UPI Safety

-   Remember: Entering your UPI PIN always means you are sending money,
    not receiving it.
-   Do not approve unknown "Collect Requests" in UPI apps.
-   Double-check recipient details before confirming any transaction.

## 🚨 Reporting & Help

-   Report cyber fraud immediately at the National Cyber Crime Portal:
    https://cybercrime.gov.in
-   Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 for urgent financial fraud
    cases.
