# Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

## ✅ Do's

-   Maintain a professional and respectful tone in your posts.
-   Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
-   Protect your privacy by adjusting security settings appropriately.
-   Use social media to build a positive digital presence (projects,
    achievements, skills).
-   Engage thoughtfully and respectfully in discussions.

## ❌ Don'ts

-   Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly.
-   Avoid posting offensive, inappropriate, or harmful content.
-   Do not engage in online arguments or toxic discussions.
-   Avoid oversharing daily activities or private matters.
-   Do not plagiarize or repost content without proper credit.
